01 — Profile

About

Financial Strategy Operational Leadership Revenue Cycle ERP Implementation Capital Governance Board Reporting Systems Thinking Team Development Mission-Driven Orgs Supply Chain

There's a version of finance that just keeps score. I've always been more interested in what the numbers are trying to say.

My job is to build the financial and operational infrastructure that lets organizations focus on what they're actually here to do. That's looked different at each stage — managing $200M in inventory through record-peak volumes at Amazon, then joining Kanad Hospital in Abu Dhabi during one of the more complex transformations a healthcare institution can go through: a full ERP from scratch, a revenue cycle redesign, and six enterprise functions brought under one financial framework for the first time.

I'm still early in a career I take seriously. I hold an MBA and Graduate Certificate in Investments from Johns Hopkins, and I'm genuinely interested in the work of building institutions that last — whether that's in healthcare, nonprofits, or wherever good organizations need someone willing to roll up their sleeves and get the foundations right.
