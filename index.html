[ 01 ] PROFILE

ABOUT

Financial Strategy Operational Leadership Revenue Cycle ERP Implementation Capital Governance Board Reporting Systems Thinking Team Development Mission-Driven Orgs Supply Chain

There's a version of finance that just keeps score. That's not what I do.

I build the financial and operational infrastructure that lets organizations focus on what they're actually here to do. That's meant different things at different stages — managing $200M in inventory through record-peak volumes at Amazon, then arriving at Kanad Hospital in Abu Dhabi in the middle of one of the most complex transformations a healthcare institution can undertake: a full ERP from scratch, a revenue cycle redesign, and six enterprise functions consolidated under one financial framework for the first time.

What's stayed consistent across both worlds is a belief that good systems make good organizations possible — and that the work of building them is worth doing carefully. I hold an MBA and Graduate Certificate in Investments from Johns Hopkins University, and I'm always thinking about the longer arc of what institutions can become when the financial foundation is right.
