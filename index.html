Currently serving as CFO at Kanad Hospital in Al Ain, UAE — providing embedded financial leadership across six enterprise functions including FP&A, revenue cycle management, patient access, supply chain, IT governance, and marketing. Accountable for an annual operating budget in the hundreds of millions of dirhams, with direct ownership of ERP platforms, capital planning, board-level reporting, and regulatory compliance under DOH and UAE frameworks.

Completing an MBA and Graduate Certificate in Investments at Johns Hopkins University.
